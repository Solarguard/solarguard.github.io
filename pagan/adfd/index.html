This page was created on August 13, 2003, collecting from the main Pagan page the files connected with the ADF Dedicants' Program. This is the very interesting introductory study program of ADF. Michael McKenny completed this in September 2000 and on being informed in July 2003 that documentation was missing, promptly replaced and rewrote it as follows below. A certificate of completion dated July 6, 2003 is now placed above an entranceway of his residence:

May what is here be interesting to whomever may read it.

Thrive,
Michael
August 13, 2003CE

1. Notes on In Search of the Indo-Europeans, by J.P. Mallory, Thames and Hudson, New York, 1991 (1989).

2.. My pagan shrine, by Michael McKenny (2000).

3. Nine Virtues, by Michael McKenny (2000).

4. PHOENIX FROM THE FLAME summary of Vivianne Crowley's awesome introduction Paganism in the 21st Century.

5. Holy Days The rough notes for the file on the eight neo-pagan holy days sent in late summer 2000 for the ADF dedicants' program.

6. Green's Journal Journal entries sent to ADF as part of the ADF Dedicants' Program.

7. Dedicants' Oath As part of the ADF Dedicants' Program the Dedicants' Oath is required. This journal entry describes the taking of the Oath in front of Red Maple Grove at the Autumnal Equinox Ritual in 2000 CE.

8. Patron As part of the ADF Dedicants' Program one describes efforts to acquire a divine patron. This file mentions the pre-existing patron relationship Green has with Oghma and Epona.

9. Rituals attended As part of the ADF Dedicants' Program one describes rituals attended during the period of study. This is the file sent in July 2003 to replace the one that had been sent in late summer 2000 CE.

10. Meditation and Two Powers A description of Green's meditation practices for the ADF Dedicants' program.

11. PAGAN CELTIC BRITAIN Michael McKenny's review of Anne Ross's challenging text.
