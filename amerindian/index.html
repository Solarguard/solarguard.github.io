Amerindians

The original inhabitants of the Americas are a diverse assembly of peoples, inheritors of attitudes, sensitivities, awarenesses that produced some of the loftiest civilizations in human history. At present, this site contains Michael McKenny's reviews of ten works and links to the very valuable Nativeweb links site. Solarguard also has a separate Canadian archaeology section. This Amerindian section was last updated on September 21, 2008 C.E. with a minor edit.

4. Colden's vol. 2 of HISTORY OF FIVE INDIAN NATIONS Summary of the second book of the 1973 edition of this significant American colonial source.
